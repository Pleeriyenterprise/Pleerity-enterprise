# Glossary

- **CRN**: Customer Reference Number – unique reference for your account (e.g. PLE-CVP-YYYY-XXXXX).
- **Requirement**: A compliance obligation tracked in the portal (e.g. Gas Safety, EICR, EPC), with a status and, where applicable, a due or expiry date.
- **Evidence**: A document or record uploaded to the portal and linked to a requirement (e.g. a gas safety certificate).
- **Vault**: Secure storage for your documents; documents can be linked to properties and requirements.
- **Portal**: The Compliance Vault Pro client area where you view properties, requirements, documents, and compliance status.

All terms above are used in the product only. They do not replace definitions in law or regulation.
