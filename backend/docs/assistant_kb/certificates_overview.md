# Certificates overview (informational only)

This is a short overview of common certificates referenced in Compliance Vault Pro. It is for information only and does not constitute legal advice.

- **Gas Safety (CP12)**: Annual gas safety check by a Gas Safe registered engineer. The certificate is valid for 12 months.
- **EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Report)**: Assessment of the electrical installation. Typically valid for 5 years (or as specified by the qualified person).
- **EPC (Energy Performance Certificate)**: Shows the energy efficiency of a property. Required for rental; minimum rating may apply depending on regulations.
- **HMO licence**: Where the property is a licensable HMO, a licence from the local authority is required. Licence conditions and duration vary by area.

For your specific obligations and deadlines, always rely on your own professional advice and the current rules that apply to your property and area.
