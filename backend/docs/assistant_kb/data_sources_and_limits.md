# Data sources and limits (informational)

The assistant uses only:

- **Portal data** – Properties, requirements, documents, and status that exist in your Compliance Vault Pro account. It does not access external databases or third-party systems.
- **This knowledge base** – Curated articles in this folder (certificates, uploads, scoring, glossary, support). Content is for information only, not legal advice.

**Limits:** The assistant does not create, edit, or delete data. It cannot see documents you have not uploaded. It cannot guarantee that the portal is complete or up to date; you are responsible for uploading and confirming evidence. If uncertain, upload or confirm evidence and seek professional advice.
